			<h1>Captain Sparklez, KSIOlajidebt, Ali-A Among New Polaris Gamers</h1>
			<div class="meta">
								<div class="details">By <a href="https://www.tubefilter.com/author/sam-gutelle/" title="Posts by Sam Gutelle" rel="author">Sam Gutelle</a> <span class="dot dot1">•</span> <span class="date">10/29/2013</span> <span class="dot">•</span></div>

											</div>
							
									<div class="articleText bottomMargin">
													<p><span style="font-size: 13px; line-height: 19px;">A flurry of activity has significantly beefed up <strong><a href="http://makerstudios.com">Maker Studios</a></strong>&#8216; most successful vertical. Four top YouTube gamers&#8211;<strong><a href="http://youtube.com/captainsparklez">Captain Sparklez</a></strong>, <strong><a href="http://youtube.com/KSIOlajidebt">KSIOlajidebt</a></strong>, <strong><a href="http://youtube.com/matroix">Ali-A</a></strong>, and <strong><a href="http://youtube.com/thebajancanadian">TheBajanCanadian</a></strong>&#8211;have joined Maker and will be listed as members of <strong><a href="http://youtube.com/polaris">Polaris</a></strong>, the network&#8217;s gaming division.</span></p>
<p>Polaris already counts a number of YouTube&#8217;s top gamers among its partners. The unstoppable Swedish force that is <a href="http://youtube.com/pewdiepie">PewDiePie</a> is well-represented on the vertical&#8217;s YouTube channel, as is top British gaming channel <a href="http://youtube.com/bluexephos">The Yogscast</a>, <a href="http://youtube.com/tobygames">Toby Turner</a>, <a href="http://youtube.com/presshearttocontinue">Dodger</a>, and many others. The four new gamers slot in nicely near the top of the vertical&#8217;s roster, and Polaris has now become the <a href="http://socialblade.com/youtube/top/networks">seventh-most popular network on YouTube</a> in terms of subscribers, despite claiming a partner count that is merely a fraction of its competitors.</p>

<p>The four new members combine to bring in more than 150 million views a month and have 13 million subscribers between them. Like any well-balanced team, they also have a variety of different specialties. Sparklez and TheBajanCanadian (real name Mitch Hughes) are partial to <em>Minecraft</em>, KSIOlajidebt (real name Jide Olatunji) is known for his <a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yQt-0QjjUKc">histrionic FIFA soccer matches</a>, and Ali-A specializes in <em>Call of Duty</em>.</p><div class="signupInline" data-label="Inline Option 1"><img src="/wp-content/themes/tubefilter/images/logo_main.svg" alt="Tubefilter"><h3>Subscribe for daily Tubefilter Top Stories</h3><span class="button">Subscribe</span></div>
<p>“As we build out our roster of gamer personalities to engage a global audience with content that appeals to everyone from hard core to casual gamers, we sought out passionate new voices and content creators,” said <strong><a href="https://twitter.com/darthefirst">Dar Nothaft</a></strong>, Vice President and general manager of Polaris.  “With Captain Sparklez, Jide, Ali-A and Mitch, we are taking Polaris to the next level.”</p>
<p>The Polaris vertical is one of a proposed 20 that will be shuffled around when <a href="http://www.tubefilter.com/2013/10/21/maker-studios-four-verticals-ynon-kreiz/">Maker re-organizes its network into four huge units</a>. With its added firepower, the gaming hub is becoming a powerful YouTube network in its own right, without even taking its prosperous parent into consideration.</p>
